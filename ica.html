The Future Democracies Laboratory @ ICA San José

An Installation by Jonathon Keats in Collaboration with Steve Durie

September 9, 2024 – February 22, 2025

The Future Democracies Laboratory is a thought-provoking installation by conceptual artist and experimental philosopher Jonathon Keats, created in partnership with Steve Durie, adjunct professor in the Digital Media Arts Program at San José State University’s CADRE Lab. This collaborative project invites visitors to critically examine the future of governance in an era increasingly shaped by artificial intelligence and digital systems.

At the center of the installation is a minimalist blue screen interface, designed to evoke the aesthetics of early computing. This seemingly simple design choice sets the tone for a participatory space—a "laboratory" for civic thought—where viewers are encouraged to engage with the core question: What role could or should technology play in our democratic processes?
